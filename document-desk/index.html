A focused review layer, when Apple Private Cloud Compute is available.

Interpretation beneath evidence—not a replacement for it.

Document Desk can send only selected, relevant grounded excerpts to Apple Private Cloud Compute for a concise review of important obligations, deadlines, fees, and risks.

  • User-triggered. Review runs only when you ask—not in the background by default.
  • Selected excerpts only. Full unrelated documents are not sent. Only the grounded claims chosen for the request.
  • Source evidence unchanged. Citations, quotes, pages, and clause identities stay as they were.
  • Interpretation beneath evidence. PCC output appears as a separate layer under the grounded brief or comparison.
  • Clear unavailable state. When Private Cloud Compute cannot be used, Document Desk fails clearly and leaves the grounded result alone.

Availability depends on device, OS, and Apple’s Private Cloud Compute path. Document Desk does not claim the review layer works on every device.
