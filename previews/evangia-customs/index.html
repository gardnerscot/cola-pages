Something Old Becomes Something You Wear

Most custom clothing starts with blank fabric. EVANGIA starts with what's already in your life — vintage quilts, unfinished cross-stitch, woven blankets, recycled textiles — and turns them into hoodies, sweatpants, shirts, jackets, and shorts you'll reach for every day.

That half-finished quilt your grandmother was working on? That's not scrap. That's the centerpiece of a hoodie that keeps her close — literally on your back.

Don't have a sentimental piece to send? No problem. Gio works from thrifted fabrics and vintage pieces too — share the vibe and he'll source, cut, and rework everything from scratch. Every piece is still one of one.

01

Your Fabric (or Mine)

Send the quilt, blanket, or textile that matters. Or just share your vision — Gio sources thrifted and vintage fabrics, then cuts and reworks everything by hand.

02

Custom Design

Pick your piece — hoodies, sweatpants, shirts, jackets, shorts. We work out the placement, fit, and how much of the original pattern shows.

03

One of One

Every piece is cut and sewn by hand. No two are the same. No templates. Just your fabric and the shape it wants to be.
