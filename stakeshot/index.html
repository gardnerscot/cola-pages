How Stakeshot Works

1

Sender Stakes Bitcoin

To message someone, you stake 1,000 sats (~$0.85). The stake is held in a Lightning wallet until the recipient responds.

2

Recipient Gets Paid

When they read your message and reply, they earn the stake. Their attention has a price — and you just paid it.

3

Spam Becomes Impossible

At $0.85 per unsolicited message, a spammer's 10-million-message campaign costs $8.5 million. Game over.
