Private by design.

LifeCapture processes your captures on-device instead of sending them to a hosted AI service.

Screenshots, receipts, notes, and voice memos are personal by nature. LifeCapture is built to keep processing local, with no LifeCapture backend receiving your captures and no subscription required to make the app useful.

  • On-device OCR and speech recognition. Extract text and transcribe voice memos on the device.
  • Local capture workflow. Import through the share sheet, camera, photos, or files and return to the app when you need the result.
  • One-time Pro upgrade. Pro is an Apple in-app purchase, not a recurring subscription.
  • No cloud promise theater. LifeCapture does not claim a remote service, hosted AI, or unrestricted cross-product search.
