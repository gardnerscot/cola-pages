Privacy Policy — LifeCapture

Last updated: July 11, 2026 · Developer: SG1 Labs (Scot Gardner)

Overview

LifeCapture is designed to keep your captures on your device. Free and Pro features process screenshots, receipts, notes, and voice memos locally.

Data stored on your device

  • Captured images, notes, transcribed audio text, OCR text, and classification metadata
  • Preferences such as Share Extension default bucket and Pro unlock state
  • Optional custom buckets and rules you create

This data lives in the app sandbox and the App Group container shared with the Share Extension and Widgets (group.com.sg1labs.lifecapture).

Data we do not collect

LifeCapture v1 does not:

  • Require an account
  • Operate a LifeCapture backend that receives your captures
  • Sell your personal data
  • Use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs

Permissions

LifeCapture may request:

  • Photos — import screenshots and images
  • Camera / Document scanner — capture receipts
  • Microphone + Speech recognition — voice memos to text
  • Notifications — optional task reminders (Pro)

You can deny permissions; related features simply stay unavailable.

Purchases

LifeCapture Pro is a one-time In-App Purchase processed by Apple. We do not receive your full payment card details. Restore Purchases uses Apple's StoreKit entitlements.

Widgets and Share Extension

Widgets and the Share Extension read/write only the shared App Group data needed to queue and display your captures. They do not transmit that data off-device.

Children's privacy

LifeCapture is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy when features change (for example, optional cloud sync). Material changes will update the date above and the App Store privacy details.

Contact

Questions about privacy: contact SG1 Labs via the support URL listed on the App Store product page.
